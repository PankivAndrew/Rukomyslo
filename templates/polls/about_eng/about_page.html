About us

The social enterprise "Рукомисли" was founded in July 2015 by the Public organization Center "Women's Perspectives" within the framework of the OSCE project on combating trafficking in human beings.

Our mission

Assistance to people from socially vulnerable categories in crisis life situations by bringing them to work activities and art therapy and promotion at employment.

The social enterprise "Handmade" works and develops in three directions:

  • garden park plastic;
  • manufacture of wool products in dry and wet felting;
  • hand painted on fabric (batik).

For this purpose training courses are constantly held, the program for which develops the art director Наталя Лєйкіна, artist and sculptor in the specialty.

Another direction of our activity is conducting master classes. We work with students aged 6-8 depending on the complexity of the program. The upper age threshold does not exist. Master classes are always held with our materials and the tools included in the cost. The theme and place of the MC are discussed in advance.

Social enterprise "Handmade" actively accepts participation in various thematic events and fairs, where you can look and buy our products.

Any item presented in the online store can be buy or order your own version in similar stylistics.
